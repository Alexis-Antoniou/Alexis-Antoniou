Game summary (text walkthrough)

You play a field service engineer at Nanoscope Corp. In the headquarters room, three researchers have a yellow “!” above their head - Dr. Priya Rao, Marcus Lindqvist and Hana Okafor. Walk up to each, press E, and pick the correct fault from a multiple-choice menu (use number keys 1–3). After all three tickets are closed, an optional bonus area appears: walk south through the door, gown up at a locker in the small gowning room, then enter the cleanroom and press E on smoking tools to bring them back online.

The game uses sight, but no fast reactions, no time limits, no flashing colours, and no audio cues are required to complete it - every event is also shown on screen as text. If you have prefers-reduced-motion set, the game pauses non-essential animations.
