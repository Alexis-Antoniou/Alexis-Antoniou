About

I am a metrology and inspection professional driven by curiosity and precision at the nanoscale.
With over a decade of hands-on expertise in Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM) probes and advanced 3D AFM imaging, I advance science and quality control in materials science, semiconductors, and next-generation inspection.

My philosophy: Every surface reveals a unique atomic story.
I provide organizations with robust analysis and innovative AFM strategies to reveal that story, supporting research, quality, and innovation.
